FAQ

Pricing & Scope

How much does a website cost?

Pricing depends on the type and complexity of the project. As a general guide:

  • Blog / Portfolio: starts from $300
  • Gallery Site: starts from $400
  • E-Commerce / Online Store: starts from $700

Every project gets a custom quote based on your specific requirements. Contact me for a free estimate — no commitment needed.

What's included in the project price?

Every project includes: design, full development, mobile responsiveness, basic on-page SEO, 2 rounds of revisions, and a handover session where I walk you through how to manage your site. Hosting, domain registration, and third-party service fees (e.g., Stripe, Mailchimp) are separate.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after launch?

Yes. I offer monthly maintenance packages covering updates, bug fixes, security patches, and minor content changes. Rates start at $80 / month. This is optional — your site is fully yours at launch whether or not you continue working with me.

Timeline & Process

How long does it take to build a website?

Typical timelines:

  • Blog / Portfolio / Gallery: 15 days-1 month business days
  • E-Commerce / Online Store: 20-30 business days

Timeline depends on how quickly you provide content (text, images, branding) and how many revision rounds are needed. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

What do you need from me to get started?

To kick off the project, I'll need:

  • A clear brief or description of what you want
  • Your logo and brand assets (if available)
  • Text / copy for the pages (or approval for me to write it)
  • Any reference sites you like the look of
  • A 50% deposit to begin

Don't worry if you're not 100% sure yet — we can work it out together in the discovery call.

Technical & Delivery

Which technologies and platforms do you use?

I choose the right tool for each project:

  • E-Commerce: Shopify, WooCommerce, or custom React + Node.js
  • Blogs / CMS: WordPress, Webflow, or Next.js + headless CMS
  • Galleries / Portfolios: Custom HTML/CSS/JS or React

I recommend the stack that fits your budget, technical comfort level, and long-term needs — not just what I prefer to build with.

Will my website be mobile-friendly and fast?

Absolutely. Every site I build is fully responsive (works on all screen sizes) and optimized for speed. I target a Google PageSpeed score of 90+ on mobile and 95+ on desktop. Fast, mobile-friendly sites rank better on Google and convert visitors more effectively.

Will I be able to update the site myself after launch?

Yes I will build for you a private backend -a page that you can manage your own website simply- and if you ordered an e-commerce we can build it depending on the discovery call and your budget -requires zero coding -.

How do we share files and communicate during the project?

We can work with whatever you're most comfortable with. For files: Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. For communication: WhatsApp, email, or LinkedIn. I send progress updates at each key stage so you're never left wondering what's happening.
